  • Research

    July, 2023-Aug, 2023

    Alarming Impact of Micrcoplastics on the reproductive system of Zebra Fish

    This study examines the impact of microplastics on fish reproductive systems in relation to plastic pollution. The researchers exposed a model fish, Danio rerio, to MP treated water from nearby plastic-polluted bodies. They also used commercially available polystyrene MPs and fluorescent PS-MPs to assess their potential impact. The findings highlight the reproductive harm microplastics may cause in fish and have implications for environmental toxicology and freshwater ecology.
